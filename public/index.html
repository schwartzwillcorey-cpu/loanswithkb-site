Loan Programs

Flexible financing options to match your situation and goals.

Traditional financing with competitive rates and flexible terms. Ideal for buyers with strong credit and stable income.

  • Down payment as low as 3% for qualified buyers
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Flexible loan terms (15, 20, 30 years)
  • No upfront mortgage insurance with 20% down

Government backed loans with lower down payment requirements. A great option for first time buyers or those rebuilding credit.

  • Down payment as low as 3.5%
  • More flexible credit requirements
  • Easier qualification for those with limited credit history
  • Available for primary residences

Exclusive benefit for veterans and active military. Often requires no down payment and offers competitive terms.

  • $0 down payment option
  • No private mortgage insurance required
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Available for eligible veterans, active duty, and qualifying spouses

Refinancing your mortgage can offer significant benefits, but it is essential to evaluate your specific circumstances and financial goals.

To help you make an informed decision, I will run a detailed break even analysis tailored to your situation. This analysis calculates how long it will take for the savings from a lower interest rate to offset the costs of refinancing.

By understanding your break even point, you can make a more confident choice about whether refinancing is the right move for you. If you have any questions or would like to get started on this analysis, feel free to reach out.
